ABOUT

My name is Marinie Ben, or Mars for short!


I am a Cambodian-American undergraduate student at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with digital and traditional experience in Architecture and Design.


I have background in studio training and professional drawing, as well as both abstract and technical thinking in design.


My focus on Architecture is the social impact it has on communities, families and individuals across the world. Whether a landmark or a private home, architecture always surrounds us as humans in society. Coming from Cambodia, a third-world country where poverty is prominent, I want to bring sustainable structures that incorporates my art to the world and can be enjoyed by all types of lives.
